🚀 The Tech Visionary: Sundar Pichai 🌐

About Him:

Sundar Pichai, born in Chennai, India, rose from humble beginnings to become the CEO of Google and Alphabet. His journey is a powerful example of ambition, intelligence, and humility.

Known for his calm demeanor and clear vision, Sundar has led groundbreaking innovations including the Chrome browser, Android OS growth, and Google AI developments. He believes in making technology universally accessible.

Under his leadership, Google continues to shape the world of search, cloud, and AI — all while advocating for digital responsibility and inclusion.
